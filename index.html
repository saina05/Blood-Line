DONATE BLOOD

Follow these four SIMPLE Steps and lend a helping hand to those in need.             SAVE LIVES

Registration (10 Mins)

Donor fills up the registration form and gives his consent for donation. It will take hardly 10 mins.

Medical Check up (5 Mins)

Donors Medical History & life style is asked, check up of temperature, blood pressure, pulse and haemoglobin.

Donation (8 Mins)

A new sterile meedle is used to draw each unit and the needle is destroyed after it has been used.

Refreshment (10 Mins)

Enjoy Snacks and drinks to replenish fluids in your body and feel contended to be able to save lives.

ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA

Age: You must be at least 17 years old to donate to the general blood supply.

Body Piercing: You must not donate if you have had a tongue, nose, belly button or genital piercing in the past 12 months.

Diabetes: Acceptable as long as it is well controlled, whether medication is taken or not

How often can you donate blood?

You can donate whole blood every 12 weeks, but you can donate plasma every 2 weeks.
